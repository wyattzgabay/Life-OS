Key Market Insight #1
Decision fatigue is the #1 reason users quit fitness apps.

Users open apps, see blank workout logs, and don't know what to do. They need prescription, not permission. The shift from "tracking tools" to "AI coaches" represents a $15B TAM expansion by 2027.
Key Market Insight #2
Premium positioning outperforms freemium in fitness.

Apps positioned as "serious tools for serious people" see 3x higher LTV despite smaller user bases. The professional demographic (30-50, $75K+ income) converts at 12% vs 4% mass market.
