PHASE 1 · CURRENT
Alpha Testing
December 2025 · 3-5 testers · $0 budget
Objectives
Primary: Validate core loop works and people actually use it daily.

Secondary: Catch critical bugs before wider distribution.

Success metric: 3+ testers using app for 7+ consecutive days.
Tester Selection
• Close friends/family who actually lift
• Mix of fitness levels (beginner to intermediate)
• At least 1 person with injury/constraint
• People who will give honest feedback
• Ideally 1 iPhone, 1 Android user
WEEK 1
Deploy & Onboard
Share PWA link, help testers install, brief walkthrough
WEEK 2
Daily Usage
Testers use app daily, you monitor feedback channel
WEEK 3
Bug Fixes
Address critical issues, push updates
WEEK 4
Debrief
Collect detailed feedback, plan beta
"The goal of alpha isn't to scale—it's to make sure the product doesn't suck before you show it to anyone who matters."
